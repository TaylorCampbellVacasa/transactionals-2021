Please note

All travelers arriving in Massachusetts must self-quarantine for 14 days, with certain exemptions.

In addition, all travelers over 18 and unaccompanied minors must complete the online Massachusetts Travel Form upon or prior to entering the state, unless you are traveling from a lower-risk state or fall within certain, limited exemption criteria. The travel form requires you to certify that you will abide by the quarantine requirements, that you have received a recent negative COVID-19 test, or that you are awaiting test results or intend to be tested upon arrival and that you will quarantine until you receive a negative test result.
